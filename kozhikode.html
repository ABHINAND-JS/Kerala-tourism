KOZHIKODE

Kozhikode also known in English as Calicut, is a city along the Malabar Coast in the state of Kerala in India. Kozhikode will always have a special place in the history of Kerala as it is here that Vasco-da-Gama first landed and the legendarySpice Route came into existence. This region still has a historic charm to it. Kozhikode Beach is the personification of the numerous characteristics that make Kozhikode so unique.
